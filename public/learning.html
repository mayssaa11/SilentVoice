Learning Space

Beginner level

  • Alphabet (A-Z)
  • Digits (1-10)
  • Basic expressions : hello, thank you, yes, no
  • Introducing your name : “My name is...”

Intermediate level

  • W-H questions : where ? when ? how ?
  • Vocabulary : family, colors, food
  • Examples : “where is the room?”, “I love apples.”

Advanced level

  • Common expressions
  • Engaging in a conversation
  • Comprehension games and challenges

About Us
