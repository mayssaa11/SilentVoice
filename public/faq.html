Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ASL?

ASL stands for American Sign Language, which is a visual language used by the deaf community in the United States and parts of Canada.

How does the ASL translator work?

The ASL translator uses video or camera input to detect sign language gestures and converts them into text in real time.

Is this app free to use?

Yes, the ASL Translator is completely free to use. We believe in making communication accessible to everyone.

Can I upload any video?

Yes, you can upload any video that shows sign language gestures. The app will process the video and provide a translation.
